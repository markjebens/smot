// BIOGRAPHY ABOUT

SINGLEMOTHEROFTWO is the DJ moniker of an artist exploring the intersection of domestic noise and functional rhythm. Specializing in structural sound design, his sets construct stripped-back, high-intensity soundscapes tailored for focused industrial environments.

Focusing on analog synthesis and percussion-driven sequencing, his performance style is defined by its metallic textures and low-frequency depth.
